What is Juldi?

Juldi is a simpler way to get to work. Just tell us where you need to go, and we'll create a shuttle route optimized for you. Whether you want to save time or just kick back and relax, Juldi is the solution to your daily commute.




Why ride with us?

Hassle Free

Let us handle the annoying parts of getting to work. You deserve to relax in our premium shuttles.

Save Time

Add hours back to your day. Get some extra work done, read that book you haven't gotten around to, or just take a well deserved nap.

Save Money

Spend less going to work. With our flat rate your costs are predictable and low - as little as $5 each way.




How does it work?

1. Sign Up

Tell us about your commute. We'll use it to make your perfect route.

2. Purchase

Purchase a pass after we launch your route. We'll send you all you need to know about timing, location, and riding.

3. Ride

Show the driver your pass and you're good to go!



Solve your commute for good.


Enter your commute information and get a custom route made for you. We'll email you as soon as we start your route. You can even take your first ride free.
