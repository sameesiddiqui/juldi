Frequently Asked Questions



How do I know I'll get to work on time?

We plan our routes so that you'll always be on time. We get you to work early, so that there is a buffer time for any delays. No matter the situation, we'll get you where you need to be.

How will I know where the pickup and dropoff points are?

We'll email you all relevant information about your route, such as the locations, times, and description of the vehicle a few days before the route is launched. We'll also send you reminders and make sure the entire process is extremely simple.

What happens if the shuttle picks me up late?

While this is extremely unlikely due to our prompt drivers and planning, in the event where you are picked up late, you'll be fully refunded and given ride credit.

What happens if I'm late?

Since there are others in the shuttle, we unfortunately cannot wait more than 2 minutes after the set departure time. This is to ensure we can get everyone to work on time. As a result, arriving late means you will need to use another form of transportation to work.

Are the drivers actual employees?

Unlike ridesharing, we have professional drivers to get you to work. Our drivers will ensure a prompt, friendly, and comfortable ride to work everyday.

Why is there only one route?

We're currently running our first pilot route to ensure our rides are consistently excellent. Once we know our customers are as happy as possible, we'll be expanding and servicing more routes.

Are the shuttles handicapped accessible?

With our current vehicles, the vans are unfortunately not handicapped accessible. If you need access to such a vehicle, the metro offers shuttles that are customized for you.
