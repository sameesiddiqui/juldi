Ride with us

Lets face it. Commuting sucks.

It's frustratingly slow, expensive, and a huge waste of time.

Juldi is here to help. We save you time and money by providing a new way to get to work.

How does it work?

We launch a shuttle route when there are enough commuters going in the same direction

Our shuttles pick you up, and take you on a direct route to your destination.

Riders split the fare - meaning you get a fast, convenient ride to work, for as little as $5.

How do I use Juldi?

1. Select the route you want

2. Purchase a pass

3. Show the driver your pass and you're good to go!
