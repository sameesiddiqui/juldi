What is Juldi?

Juldi is a faster and cheaper way to get to work. We group you with nearby commuters going in the same direction, and create a shuttle route that's customized for you.

Whether you want to spend less, maximize your day, or just simplify your life, Juldi makes it possible.

How does it work?

Once you tell us where you need to go, we determine the most direct route to get you there.

After you have a route, our shuttles will pick you up every day, and take you to your destination

Riders split the fare - meaning you get a fast, convenient ride to work, for as little as $5.

How do I sign up?

1. Enter your commute information

2. Purchase a pass when we launch your route

3. Show the driver your pass and you're good to go!
