What is Juldi?

Juldi takes you to work on a personalized shuttle route driven by a professional. Just tell us where you need to go, and we'll handle the rest.


Why ride with us?

Hassle Free

Let us handle the annoying parts of getting to work. You deserve to relax in our premium shuttles.

Save Time

Add hours back to your day. Get some extra work done, read that book you haven't gotten around to, or just take a well deserved nap.

Save Money

Spend less going to work. With our flat rate your costs are predictable and low.
